TINY HOME

Welcome to our small format modular container homes, where innovative design meets sustainable living. Crafted from upcycled raw materials, our bespoke container homes offer an inviting and warm aesthetic, perfect for a variety of settings.

Whether you envision a cozy granny flat nestled in suburban tranquility, a serene getaway in the rolling hills, or a minimalist tiny house in the picturesque countryside, our designs cater to your unique needs.

The Containable Tiny Home's compact yet sophisticated design includes a luxurious bathroom with a spacious shower, toilet, and vanity with ample storage. Upon entering through the four sliding doors, you are welcomed into a fully equipped kitchen featuring generous storage, extensive counter space, and modern appliances ideal for meal preparation. The adjacent living area offers flexible options for seating and additional bench space, enhancing comfort and functionality. Beyond the kitchen and living area lies the bedroom, furnished with a king-size bed and optimized for storage with under-bed compartments and headboard cabinetry, accommodating all clothing and personal essentials. Large corner windows in the bedroom provide stunning panoramic views from dawn to dusk. Additionally, the home offers the opportunity for a large open deck, perfect for enjoying country living or urban luxury.
